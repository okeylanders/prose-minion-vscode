# Writing Guides

This folder groups longer, full writing guides and short descriptor snippets for quick reference.

## Structure
- guides/ — Longer, full guides (expanded "show, don't tell" resources)
- snippets/ — Short lists and descriptor snippets (quick examples, expression-shifted notes)

## Guides (long-form)
- guides/body-movement-action.md — Full-body movement and stance; arms, legs, torso, head, micro-movements, figurative language.
- guides/school-day-student-life.md — Classroom, lockers, lunchtime, and student micro-expressions.
- guides/basketball-game.md — Pre-game atmosphere, player movement, crowd energy, and emotional beats.
- guides/prom-night.md — Prom set-up, arrivals, atmosphere, dance and traditions.
- guides/art-class.md — High-school art classroom environment, supplies, instruction, and emotional coloration.
- guides/car-ride.md — Four-occupant car ride dynamics, environment, and interactions.
- guides/camping-tent.md — Three friends setting up a tent: selection, setup, banter, and environment.
- guides/contemporary-church-service.md — Modern worship service: lobby, worship flow, micro-gestures, and atmosphere.
- guides/campfire-stories.md — Campfire spooky-tale atmosphere, pacing, sensory cues, and reactions.
- guides/sonicscapes-environmental-sounds.md — Creating immersive sonic environments without weather-based pathetic fallacy.
