# Spatial Descriptors

## Categories

### Space as Container

- silence filled every corner
- absence left a vacuum
- music occupied completely
- grief pressed everyone to edges
- tension condensed the air
- light saturated the room
- fog inundated the valley
- presence filled like water finding places

### Space as Medium

- melody wound through space
- words hung suspended
- light scattered through room
- sound carried across auditorium
- colors bled into one another
- incense spiraled through chapel
- smoke threaded through crowd
- silence diffused through space
