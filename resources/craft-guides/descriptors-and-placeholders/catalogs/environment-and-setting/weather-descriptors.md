# Weather Descriptors

## Categories

### Storm Progression

- living thing of wind and fury
- sagged into exhausted persistence  
- storm had spent itself
- mechanical patience of wipers
- rhythm becoming meditation
- flooded streets like ark navigation
- downpour carving weak paths
- storm-blurred darkness
- defensive shadows of shuttered homes
- rain that transforms light

### Storm Sounds

- whoosh, swish rhythmic meditation
- wipers moving with mechanical patience
- each sweep revealing more darkness
- rhythm itself as meditation
- silence lingering longer between sounds
- collective exhales fogging windows
- tires splashing through pooled water
- steady rain bombardment
- umbrella forgotten too late
- storm sounds as sanctuary

### Post-Storm Atmosphere

- exhausted persistence of weather
- mechanical patience after fury
- flooded streets reflecting light
- pooled water in driveways
- steady rain replacing downpour
- storm-spent calm settling
- water everywhere but manageable
- transition from chaos to order
- gentle rain on sanctuary roof
- aftermath creating intimacy

### Storm as Character

- storm spending itself like energy
- fury transforming to persistence
- weather as living entity
- rain as baptismal transition
- storm as exodus from danger
- flooding as passage through trial
- emergence into sanctuary
- storm as divine intervention
- weather marking narrative beats
- atmospheric transformation

### Rain and Light Interaction

- headlights carving weak paths
- darkness revealed by each sweep
- storm-blurred revelations
- amber-hallelujah through rain
- light fighting storm then yielding
- warm yellows through water
- illumination softened by moisture
- golden rectangles through rain
- sanctuary light vs storm darkness
- rain as lens transforming vision
