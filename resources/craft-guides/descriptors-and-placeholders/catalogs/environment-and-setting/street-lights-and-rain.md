# Street Lights and Rain Descriptors

## Categories

### Atmospheric Descriptive

- amber streetlights
- orange-tinted halos
- sulfur-yellow pools
- sodium vapor glow
- amber sodium wash
- tangerine streetlamps
- honey-colored light rings

### More Poetic and Literary

- broken halos of municipal light
- fractured amber coronas
- municipal amber bleeding across wet pavement
- sodium's jaundiced glow
- streetlight bruises in amber and rust
- the city's sickly orange heartbeat
- pools of artificial sunset

### Sensory and Tactile

- light that tasted of rust and electricity
- amber light thick as syrup
- sodium glow pooling like warm honey
- streetlight amber bleeding into rain-slick asphalt
- orange light that felt heavy, almost liquid
- the corridor's amber pulse
- sodium vapor settling like dust motes in amber

### Weather Integrated

- sodium lights haloed by rain
- amber reflections shattered across puddles
- streetlight orange smeared by windshield wipers
- sodium glow diffused through storm mist
- amber pools collecting in tire ruts
- the corridor's amber spine stretching through rain
