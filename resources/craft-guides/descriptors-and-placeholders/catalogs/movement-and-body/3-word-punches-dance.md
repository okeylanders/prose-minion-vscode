# 3-Word Punches for Dance

## Usage Examples

### Integrated into Prose

> "Kiiara, anyone?" Prim laughed, "Paaajaaammma Dance Party!" **Rise. Reach. Rally.** The motion flowed like choreography—theatrical flourish transitioning to outstretched invitation.

> The music hit and suddenly it was **pulse-pivot-pose**, everyone finding their rhythm in the sanctuary space.

> What started tentative became confident: **gather-groove-grow**, the living room transforming into their private dance floor.

> Kayla's movement was pure invitation: **bounce-beam-beguile**, drawing even the reluctant into the celebration.

> The group found their collective rhythm: **sync-sway-synergy**, bodies speaking the language of shared joy.

### Standalone Descriptors

- **Flow. Flourish. Freeze.** — for graceful, theatrical moments
- **Burst. Boom. Blaze.** — for explosive, energetic dancing
- **Glide. Grace. Glow.** — for elegant, fluid movement
- **Pop. Punch. Power.** — for sharp, rhythmic dancing
- **Drift. Dance. Dream.** — for ethereal, floating movement

## Notes on Usage

- Use periods between words for staccato effect
- Use hyphens for flowing connection
- Can be used as internal rhythm or external description
- Effective as transition between longer prose passages
- Best when matched to character personality and scene energy
