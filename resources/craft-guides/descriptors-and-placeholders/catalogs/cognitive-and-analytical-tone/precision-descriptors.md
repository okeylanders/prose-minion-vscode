## Show Dont Tell

### Instead of: "He moved with precision."

**Try:**

- He rose from the sectional with characteristic deliberateness.
- Each gesture was measured, calculated.
- His movements had a geometric precision, reflecting years of careful formation.
- He navigated the crowded room with an economy of motion, never jostling a soul.
- Every step was placed with intentionality, as if he were walking on a sacred map.

---

### Instead of: "She spoke with precision."

**Try:**

- Every word was chosen with a linguistic accuracy that bordered on the academic.
- Her diction was measured, each syllable given its proper weight.
- "It's theological nomenclature," she replied, her voice clear and exact.
- She articulated her argument with rhetorical exactness, leaving no room for misinterpretation.
- Her sentences were models of syntactic clarity, clean and direct.

---

### Instead of: "He worked with precision."

**Try:**

- He approached the task with a methodical attention to detail.
- His work was a study in practiced efficiency.
- He arranged the objects with a careful, almost reverent, consideration for their placement.
- His systematic approach left nothing to chance.

---

### Instead of: "Her observation was precise."

**Try:**

- She observed him with a casual precision, cataloging each gesture.
- Her analysis was clinical, her conclusions drawn with diagnostic accuracy.
- She examined the object with deliberate scrutiny, missing no detail.
- Her assessment was methodical, breaking the problem down into its smallest parts.
