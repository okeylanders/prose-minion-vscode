# Communication Descriptors

- entire paragraphs passed between them

- fingers pressing lightly against sleeve

- bumped shoulders in familiar way

- single syllable carrying multiple layers

- automatic sideways shift
- unconscious ceremony preserved
- creating space for absent person
- telltale sign of thinking

- voice never leaving subject
