# ANALYSIS FOCUS: Dialogue Line Refinement

For this analysis, prioritize dialogue line improvements:

## PRIMARY EMPHASIS (80% of suggestions)

- **Subtext and implied meaning**: What's being said beneath the surface
- **Character voice consistency**: Each speaker's unique diction, rhythm, and verbal patterns
- **Rhythm, cadence, and flow**: How the lines sound when spoken aloud
- **Emotional authenticity**: Lines that feel true to the character's emotional state

## SECONDARY (20% of suggestions)
